Welcome to the Ultimate adventure!






What We Offer!


Unltimate Adventures is a one-of-a-kind overnight summer camp for kids ages 8-16, in upper arizona near sedona. The ultimate adventure experience is unique: campers choose a camp activity focus for their mornings and all of their own afternoon fun for a personalized adventure.

Our campers follow their dreams, try incredible new things and gain lifelong friends in a safe and supportive environment. Send your child to the place where they will grow, feel successful and return home saying, “Ultimate Adventure is the best camp ever!”








Activities!


At Ultimate Adventures, we provide you kid with a assortment of fun activities. They are meant to keep you kid in action at all times, and for them to have fun. Some of the activities we offer are:

  • Water sports
  • Hiking
  • Archery
  • Obsticle courses
  • Fishing








Yous Campers Safety is our priority!


At Ultimate Adventures, Your Campers safety is our number 1 priority. Your child will be will camp directors to ensure that nothing bad happens. Our camp directors are all first aid train if something bad were to happen.
